Total Complaints, Three-Year Trend

Complaints received across ORBIO offices and the Centralised Receipt and Processing Centre have grown sharply since the Integrated Ombudsman Scheme's first full year of operation.

Category Share of Complaints

Loans and Advances has remained the largest single category across all three years on record here, while Credit Card has climbed steadily and overtook Mobile / Electronic Banking by FY24–25. Bars show each category's share of total complaints for that year, not its year-on-year growth rate (Credit Card complaints grew 20.04% year-on-year in FY24–25, but make up 17.15% of total complaints that year). FY22–23 bars (lighter, hatched) are calculated by summing entity-level complaint counts from that year's report, since RBI did not publish a single overall share per category that year — see SOURCES.md for the calculation.
