# Data Sources

All figures in this project were read directly from RBI's official Annual
Reports of the Ombudsman Scheme, downloaded from rbi.org.in and personally
verified against the source tables. No complaint-level or individual
complainant data is used anywhere in this project, since RBI does not
publish that publicly. Only aggregate, already-published statistics appear
in the dashboard.

**Important correction:** an earlier version of this project listed Credit
Card share for FY24-25 as 20.04%. That figure is actually the year-on-year
growth rate for the Credit Card category, not its share of total
complaints. The correct share is 17.15% (50,811 complaints), as shown
above. This was caught and fixed by reading the primary source table
directly rather than relying on secondary news reporting.

**Note on FY2022-23:** the FY22-23 report does not publish a single
"share of total complaints" figure per category the way the FY23-24 and
FY24-25 reports do. Instead, it breaks each category down separately by
entity type (banks, NBFCs, payment system participants, and credit
information companies), each with its own percentage of that entity's
total. To produce a comparable figure for the three-year trend, the
entity-level complaint counts for each category were summed and divided
by RBI's published ORBIO total for FY22-23 (2,34,690 complaints). This
calculation is shown below, clearly separated from the directly-published
figures for FY23-24 and FY24-25.

**FY 2022-23 — calculated from entity-level data (not directly published by RBI as a single figure):**

| Category | Complaints (summed across entity types) | Calculated Share |
|---|---|---|
| Loans and Advances | 58,990 (39,579 banks + 18,657 NBFCs + 754 CICs) | ≈25.14% |
| Mobile / Electronic Banking | 42,101 (39,855 banks + 2,246 PSOs/PSPs) | ≈17.94% |
| Credit Cards | 24,612 (24,549 banks + 63 CICs) | ≈10.49% |

The sum of all entity-level complaint counts in the FY22-23 report
(2,34,202) reconciles closely with RBI's published ORBIO total for that
year (2,34,690), a difference of 488 complaints (0.2%), which is treated
as rounding or a small unbroken-out category and does not materially
affect the calculated shares above. Full entity-by-entity source figures
are in Table 1.5 of the FY22-23 report.

## What is NOT real data in this project

The `classifier/` component uses synthetic, author-written complaint-style
text samples to demonstrate an NLP classification pipeline against RBI's
published complaint categories. RBI does not publish individual complaint
narratives publicly, so no real complainant text exists anywhere in this
project. This is stated clearly in `classifier/README.md` and in the
project's main README.

## Verification method

All figures above were extracted directly from the three PDF reports using
text extraction and manually cross-checked against the executive summary
narrative and the corresponding chart or table in each report. This was
done after an earlier version of this project had compiled figures via
secondary news reporting; rebuilding against the primary source directly
caught and corrected one error (the Credit Card FY24-25 share, noted
above).
